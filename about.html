Our Mission

We Connect The "Little Guy" With The Big Tax Breaks.

TallyHelper was built on a simple premise: Most small business owners are overpaying the IRS because their general accountant is too busy to be a specialist.

The Generalist Trap

The US Tax Code is over 17,000 pages long. Expecting a local CPA to master every niche credit—like the FICA Tip Credit (IRC §45B)—is unrealistic. As a result, billions of dollars earmarked for the hospitality and service industries go unclaimed every year.

The Marketplace Solution

TallyHelper is not an accounting firm. We are a specialized marketplace. We have vetted dozens of tax firms across the country and selected only those that meet our strict criteria: Specialization, Technology, and Audit Defense.
