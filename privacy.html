Last Updated: January 13, 2026

We collect information to facilitate the assessment process. This includes your contact information (name, email, phone number) and business details (business type, employee count, and tax classification) necessary to determine potential eligibility for the FICA Tip Tax Credit.

We use your information to assess your qualification for tax credits and to facilitate communication regarding your potential refund. By submitting your information, you authorize us to share your details with these partners so they may contact you to discuss your tax credit recovery options.
